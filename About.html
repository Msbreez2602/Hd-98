Hi, My name is Brianne, or Bre for short! I'm currently a senior at Oakland University studying graphic design. I enjoy creating logos, branding propoals and posters.

When I'm not doing such, I enjoy playing either my bass guitar, piano, or singing. I'm apart of an amazing band called Confidence, lead by Rockim Williamson, where we've performed at various events including the infamous Arts Beats and Eats in Royal Oak!

When it comes to art, I love exploring the endless possibilities of drawing, painting, resin art and spray painting! When I graduate from Oakland University, I plan to leave my mark in the world by wokring for major companies to inspire creativity in branding; as well as work on my business, Holt Designs, to satisfy my passion of creating life-changing and eye-catching brands and logos.
